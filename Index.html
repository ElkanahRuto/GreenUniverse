AND AGRICULTURE
(YOCAA)

YFCAAD - TREE PLANTING PROJECT

GOAL

To build a climatically sustainable future though mobilizing school communities to plant trees.

OBJECTIVES

To promote access to healthy diets both in primary and secondary schools.
To relay environmental education to learners, teachers and the entire community.
To build a climatically sustainable environment for the community.

PURPOSE

To combat climate change

ACTIVITIES

Tree planting
Environment awareness
Demonstrations
Monitoring

BENEFITS

A long-term conservation of the environment.
The school children and the people within the school communities will benefit from fruits’ nutritional values.
The multipurpose trees and fruits will not only be the source of food, also, they will be the major provider of shades.
The project will enhance children’s environmental awareness as they will build their capacity to nurture trees rightfully.
Enhanced income for the school communities through sale of seedlings and fruits.

Our Partners

Global Giving
